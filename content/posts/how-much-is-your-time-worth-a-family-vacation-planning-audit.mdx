## When DIY makes sense (and when it doesn't)

Full transparency: not everyone should hire travel planning services. If you genuinely enjoy researching flight routing and have flexible time, planning your own trips can be rewarding.

**DIY works when:**
- You have 15+ hours available for thorough research
- You travel frequently enough to understand airline alliance benefits and hotel elite status perks
- Your hourly value sits below $50 and opportunity cost remains low
- You're planning simple domestic trips like Los Angeles to San Francisco with standard hotel requirements

**Professional booking makes sense when:**
- Your hourly value exceeds $75 and potential time savings from DIY research
- You're planning complex international travel involving connecting flights and visa requirements
- You have specific American Express Fine Hotels & Resorts or Virtuoso benefits to leverage
- You want professional recourse when United cancels flights or Marriott oversells rooms

The decision becomes obvious once you calculate your actual hourly worth and compare it to the 10-20 hour time investment required for thorough trip planning.
